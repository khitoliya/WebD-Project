About:

Delhi Technological University (DTU), which was established in 1941. Initially situated at Kashmere Gate, we offered a diverse range of programs. In 1952, we became affiliated with the University of Delhi and later contributed to the formation of institutions like the School of Planning and Architecture and the College of Arts.

In 2009, we transitioned to DTU, gaining more autonomy. In 1996, we relocated to a 164-acre campus in Rohini, which has furthered our focus on research and innovation. DTU continues to excel in engineering and technology education.
